Business Health Diagnosis

Audit Mandiri Risiko Hukum Anda

1. Licensing Risk

Have you reviewed your business licenses (NIB, operational permits, sectoral permits) within the last 12 months to ensure they are still valid and compliant?

2. Food Safety Compliance

Do you have documented SOPs for food handling, storage temperature, sanitation, and employee hygiene that are regularly updated?

3. Inspection Preparedness

If a surprise inspection from local authorities occurred tomorrow, are you 100% confident you would pass without warnings or sanctions?

4. Supplier & Liability

Do you have written contracts with suppliers clearly allocating liability for contaminated or defective products?

5. Complaint Protection

If a customer claims food poisoning or hygiene-related harm, do you have a legal response protocol in place?

Risiko Terdeteksi: Anda menjawab "NO" pada salah satu poin di atas. Bisnis Anda rentan secara hukum.
